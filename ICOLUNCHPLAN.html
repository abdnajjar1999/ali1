ICO Launch Plan

The launch of the project's digital currency will follow two main phases, each divided into two sub-phases. This approach ensures transparency and attracts investors by building trust and confidence in the project's concept.

Phase 1: Pre-ICO

This phase aims to lay the groundwork for the project and establish initial market interest. Duration: 365 days

PRE-ICO 1
  • Token Units: 1 billion units
  • Price per Token: $0.20 USD
  • Target: $200 million USD
PRE-ICO 2
  • Token Units: 2 billion units
  • Price per Token: $0.40 USD
  • Target: $800 million USD

Phase 2: Main ICO

This phase focuses on scaling the project and preparing the currency for listing on trading platforms.

MAIN-ICO 1
  • Token Units: 3 billion units
  • Price per Token: $0.80 USD
  • Target: $2.4 billion USD
MAIN-ICO 2

  • Target: $4 billion USD
